# Task 1 Scoring Guide

Use this repository-owned guide for educational score estimates. Do not quote or reproduce official descriptor text in generated output.

## Criteria

- Task Achievement: answer the visual task, include a clear overview, select important features, report accurate data, and avoid unsupported interpretation.
- Coherence & Cohesion: group information logically, sequence comparisons clearly, use cohesive devices naturally, and paragraph the report appropriately.
- Lexical Resource: use precise visual/data vocabulary, vary comparison language, avoid repetition, and maintain formal academic wording.
- Grammatical Range & Accuracy: use controlled sentence structures, accurate articles/plurals/prepositions, correct tense, and reliable punctuation.

## Practical Band Anchors

- Band 8 range: clear overview, skilful key-feature selection, accurate and well-grouped details, flexible vocabulary, mostly error-free grammar.
- Band 7 range: relevant and accurate overall, clear overview, generally logical grouping, some flexibility in vocabulary and grammar, but occasional lapses or underdeveloped comparisons.
- Band 6 range: task is mostly addressed, overview may be weak or generic, data selection is uneven, organisation is understandable, and language errors/repetition are noticeable.
- Band 5 range: limited overview, mechanical listing, important features missed or misread, weak grouping, restricted vocabulary, and frequent language errors.
- Below Band 5: serious task misunderstanding, little relevant data, poor organisation, or errors that frequently block meaning.

## Review Use

- Score the student's original answer, not the rewritten version.
- Use half bands when the answer sits between two practical anchors.
- Comment on Task Achievement issues before polishing grammar.
- Band 7.5 rewrites should be accurate, concise, and learnable.
- Band 8 model answers should be strong but realistic, not over-complex.
