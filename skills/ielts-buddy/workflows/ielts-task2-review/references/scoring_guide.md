# Task 2 Scoring Guide

Use this repository-owned guide for educational score estimates. Do not quote or reproduce official descriptor text in generated output.

## Criteria

- Task Response: answer every part of the prompt, present a clear position where required, develop relevant ideas, and avoid memorised or off-topic material.
- Coherence & Cohesion: organise ideas into logical paragraphs, maintain progression, connect ideas naturally, and avoid mechanical or excessive linking.
- Lexical Resource: use precise topic vocabulary, natural collocations, appropriate academic register, and enough range without forcing rare words.
- Grammatical Range & Accuracy: use varied sentence forms with control, keep punctuation reliable, and prevent repeated errors from distracting the reader.

## Practical Band Anchors

- Band 8 range: fully relevant response, clear and well-developed position, well-extended support, smooth progression, flexible vocabulary, and mostly error-free grammar.
- Band 7 range: addresses the task clearly, develops a position, supports main ideas, organises paragraphs logically, and shows language flexibility with some lapses.
- Band 6 range: addresses the main task but some parts are thinner, support may be general, progression is understandable, and vocabulary/grammar errors are noticeable.
- Band 5 range: task coverage is incomplete, ideas are limited or repetitive, paragraphing and cohesion are weak, vocabulary is restricted, and errors often distract.
- Below Band 5: serious task misunderstanding, little direct response, unclear position, poor organisation, or errors that frequently block meaning.

## Review Use

- Score the student's original essay, not the rewritten version.
- Use half bands when the essay sits between two practical anchors.
- Mark off-topic content and weak task response before style or grammar polish.
- Band 7.5 rewrites should be concise, formal, and close to the student's intended meaning.
- Band 8 model essays should be strong but realistic, not over-polished.
